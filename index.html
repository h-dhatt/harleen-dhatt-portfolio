About

I like analytics that feels useful: definitions are crisp, numbers reconcile, and the “so what?” is obvious.

My approach is simple: start with the decision, build trustworthy data behind it, and communicate results in a way a busy reader can absorb in one pass.

I’m especially drawn to retention + growth questions (funnels, cohorts, experiments), and to the unglamorous work that makes everything reliable (cleaning rules, QA checks, and reproducible analysis).
